Projec-T is a project focused on creating graphic T-shirts,
treating the T-shirt as a blank canvas to design and apply various graphics.
All designs were limited to flat graphics,
emphasizing how the shapes and compositions
of the designs could be expressed on the T-shirt.
All products are conceptual and not available for purchase.
